Reproductive Health Education

Our programs focus on educating young girls and boys on reproductive health, discussing the consequences of undesired pregnancies, methods to prevention , and combating Gender-Based Violence (GBV). We also provide vital information on HIV, STIs, and the importance of social development.

TUYISENGE Tv-KARAME MUBYEYI.

Our online Tv channel is dedicated to empowering the Rwandan community, Tuyisenge Tv-karame mubyeyi offers invaluable educational content on sexual reproductive health, all in the native Kinyarwanda language. Our channel aims to provide accurate information, address myths, and promote safe practices, ensuring that everyone has access to the knowledge they need for informed decisions about their health. Join us as we delve into essential topics, fostering a well-informed and healthy community.
