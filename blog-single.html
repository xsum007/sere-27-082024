KARAME MUBYEYI, like other Non-Governmental Organizations either Local or International social and health institutions, we aim on achieving sustainable results from developmental activities and initiatives to our communities, especially with the youth, women, widows and the disable

Our mission is building the family Health Capacity and promoting health preventive information for crippling labor death rate

KARAME MUBYEYI provides services in Rehabilitation of teen mothers, training on breastfeeding, children’s nutrition management, reintegration in the society and their family, Advocacy, contributing to development and wellbeing communities’ needs.

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES 1. To raise awareness, understanding and knowledge on SRHR and other REPRODUCTIVE related CANCER within the district regions and Rwanda in general. 2. To promote gender equalities in education by achieving equity in family development, with ensuring access to teen pregnancy. 3. To make campaigns of SRHR to head of families for parent-child communication encouragement. 4. Empower teen mothers to be financially independent through capacity building. 5. Empower youth to take life seriously within the region rehabilitation.

AREA OF OPERATION: We operate in Western province of Rwanda, covering so many sector in that area of district and its environs

TARGET GROUP: The primary target of the is teen mothers, adolescent girls, young pregnant and avoid street kids, people living with HIV and AIDS, people with disabilities, widows and youths
