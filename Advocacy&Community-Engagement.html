Advocacy & Community-Engagement

We stand up for the rights of young girls who've been ostracized from their families due to unwanted pregnancies. By engaging in advocacy from the grassroots to the national levels, we ensure their voices are heard, their rights are upheld, and they are reintegrated into their families and communities.
